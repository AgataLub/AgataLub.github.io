About us

Hello there, fellow food-lover!

We are the Food Finders, a group of students and food enthusiast based in Copenhagen. We are young, international and ready to take you on a taste trip throughout the city.

Julita, alias "Sushi Explorer", loves to try new Sushi restaurants every now and then. If you're into Sushi she can tell you where to go for a delicious and unforgettable night.

Claudia is the Dane of the group. She knows all the apps where to get discounts on food as a student in Copenhagen and she'll share with you all her knowledge, tips and tricks.

Laura loves eating, but hates cooking. She knows where to get ready-to-eat food, so you won't have to touch a pan ever again!

Agata is our Ramen Conossieur. Go to her to get to know the best ramen places in town and the best deals.
